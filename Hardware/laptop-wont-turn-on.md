# Troubleshooting a Laptop That Will Not Turn On

## Symptoms
- Laptop does not power on
- No lights or screen activity
- Black screen when pressing power button

## Possible Causes
- Dead battery
- Faulty power adapter
- Hardware failure
- Loose internal components

## Steps to Resolve

### 1. Check Power Source
- Ensure power adapter is plugged in
- Test with a known working outlet
- Verify charging indicator light

### 2. Perform Power Reset
- Disconnect charger
- Hold power button for 15–30 seconds
- Reconnect power and try again

### 3. Test with Different Charger
- Use a compatible charger if available
- Check for damaged cables

### 4. Remove External Devices
- Disconnect USB devices, docking stations, peripherals
- Attempt to power on

### 5. Check for Signs of Life
- Listen for fan noise
- Look for LED indicators
- Check screen brightness

### 6. Test External Display
- Connect to external monitor
- Determine if display is the issue

### 7. Inspect for Physical Damage
- Check for liquid damage, cracks, or overheating

## Resolution
Power restored after identifying faulty charger or performing power reset.

## Follow-Up Actions
- Replace faulty hardware if needed
- Document issue and resolution
- Escalate for hardware repair if required

## Notes
If no signs of power are present, device may require professional repair or replacement.
